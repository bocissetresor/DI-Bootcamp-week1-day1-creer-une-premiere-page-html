About domestic cats

They are often called house cats when kept as indoor pets or simply cats when there is no need to distinguish them from other felids and felinex. They are often valued by humans for companionship.

there are more than seventy cat breeds recognized by various cat registries

Cat senses

  • They can hear sounds too faint or too high in frequency for human ears
  • They can see in near darkness
  • They have poorer color vision and a better sense of smell than humans.

History

Cats were venerated in ancien Egypt, but there may have been instances of domestication as early as the Neolithie from around 9,500 years ago (7500>BC).
